**Turn any walk into an adventure.**

SpotIt World is an AI-powered Android application that generates unique, location-aware scavenger hunts for children and families. Whether you are at Sheffield Park or a sidewalk in the city, SpotIt World creates a bingo-style grid of items to find, tailored to your surroundings and the age of your explorers.

## The Vibe ‚ú®
- **Active Play:** Getting kids off screens and into the real world.
- **Infinite Content:** No two hunts are ever the same.
- **Educational:** Learn about local flora, fauna, and urban features.

## Core Features
- **AI Generation:** Uses LLMs to analyze location descriptions and generate relevant items.
- **Difficulty Scaling:** From "Find a red leaf" (Toddler) to "Find a Quercus Robur" (Expert).
- **Bingo Mode:** Complete lines or full houses to score points.

## Tech Stack
- **Language:** Kotlin
- **UI:** Jetpack Compose
- **Architecture:** Clean Architecture (MVVM + Repository Pattern)
- **Local Data:** Room Database
- **Remote Data:** AI Integration (OpenAI/Gemini)
